About the studio


DeValiance is a small indie game development studio based in Mauritius. The studio was founded on 30 November 2015 by Sarvesh Gopee, alias MisterValiant. The studio specialises in making fun and free to play family-friendly games on the windows and android platform. The business also empowers indie developers by providing starter game development resources.

Our slogan is, “Together, we are Valiant!”. We aim at bridging the gap between gamers and developers.
