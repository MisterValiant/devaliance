Our Games

Every DeValiance game shares an ambition to break down barriers and inspire long-lasting, positive connections with players, wherever they are in the world.

The Valiant Cube

The Valiant Cube is a fun fast-paced runner game developed in collaboration with the gaming community.
This is the first game DeValiance's community created together and it really shows how much our indie community is passionate about the art of game development. The game features a cube sliding through the different levels in order to reach its destination.

Valiant Kart

Hop on a kart and go on an epic adventure with your favourite friend, Valiant! The game features a lot of themes and stages adapted from popular games.

Come experience some of our communites best creations in the brand new battle mode.

Super Valiant

Valiant is back on another adventure far from the Mushroom Biome.

Dowser has again kidnapped the princess!
Help our hero in navigating the different stages, defeat mobs and bosses, and save our dear princess Amethyst.

Valirant

Technological terrors has raided all over Valiantia and destroying every single buidling in the biome. Hop in this chaotic world and experience a face-paced tactical shooter game in the scary lairs of the invaders. Help our hero, Valiant, with his zero-ray gun, restore peace in the mushroom biome again.
