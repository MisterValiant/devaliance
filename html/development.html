What are we up next?
DeValiance is constantly working hard to bring the best out of its employees and stakeholders. The team has been organising a lot of events to empower both gamers and developers for the betterment of both the gaming industry and humanity. Some of our successful projects are:


MetaHuman Creator
By harvesting the computing power of the unreal engine, we learnt how to create super realistic human models and implement them in short game projects.
Valiant Earth
Our community came up together to form the non-profit organisation, Valiant Earth. We teach people how to invest in technology to improve quality of life, help them in building a career and save the planet at very affordable prices.
