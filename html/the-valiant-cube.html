THE VALIANT CUBE

Come slide your way through the different stages crafted by our community in this casual fast-paced runner game.

ABOUT THE GAME

Slide through stages!
The Valiant Cube is a game categorised in the gaming industry as a running genre video game and is created by both our developers at DeValiance and our awesome community.

This projet has been a really big stepping stone in the collaboration of our communities. We hope you will enjoy the game as much as we loved developing it for you!

Gameplay

Slide your way

The game has a lot of stages created by gamers just like you!
The Valiant Cube is designed to be played both casually and competitively among friends.

Beware of blocks!

Are you up for the challenge?
Your aim is to reach the finish line by avoiding all obstacles placed along the way.
Be careful not to fall out of the map!

Endless fun

Explore the infinite challenge and get as far as you can.
Compare your highscore with other players and make your way through the leaderboard.
