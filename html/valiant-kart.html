Valiant Kart

Hop into a kart's seat with fantastic tracks and features and duke it out in a never seen battle mode.

ABOUT THE GAME

Race & battle!
Hit the road with Valiant Kart anytime and anywhere. Race your friends or compete in the new battle mode with our bots.
Our developers at DeValiance has put a lot of effort into this game. Every track, theme, music and other features makes the game glorious and a must play.
Support the game by sharing it with your loved ones.

Gameplay

3, 2, 1, GO!

Select a kart, start the engines, hit a level and battle on the different courses such as starship valley and Hyrule island.

Battle bots

Can you keep up and cross the finish line?
Challenge our battle bots or even your friends in a brand new battle mode.
Spice the game up with a combination of time attack and hardcore bots.

Explore!

Play on our various valiant maps crafted both by gamers and developers at DeValiance.
Theme parks, castles and space centres! Our team got you covered.
