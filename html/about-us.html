Our Story

DeValiance is a small indie game development studio based in Mauritius.
The studio was founded on 30 November 2015 by Sarvesh Gopee, alias MisterValiant.
The studio specialises in making fun and free to play family-friendly games on the windows and android platform. The business also empowers indie developers by providing starter game development resources.

Gaming

Most of us come from a gaming background with years of experience in the field of content creation. We have learnt the different techniques of video and audio editing, image manipulation and even some digital marketing.

Tech enthusiast

Passionate about technology, the team aims at using all the resources possible to satisfy our users and innovate.
Our team also regularly hop on projects and other challenges to deliver the best products and services to our valiant community.
