Super Valiant

Embark on a captivating adventure in this platform game and save the princess from the clutches of Dowser.

ABOUT THE GAME



Jump and Squash!
Explore incredible places far from the Mushroom biome as you join Valiant on a massive adventure to save Princess Amethyst.
Uncover loads of locations and secrets and break the limits in our infinite challenge mode.

Gameplay

Don't fall!

Run, jump and squash your way through the levels. But!
Be careful not to fall down the ravines.

Squash!

There is nothing more satisfying than watching slimes getting squashed.
However,
be careful not to touch their sides.

Secrets!

The developers created the stages but, the mysteries were kept deep in the dungeons by our community.
Try to find them all.
